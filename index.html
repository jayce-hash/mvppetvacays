Good to Know

Common Questions

Which pets do you care for?

Dogs, cats, and other household pets. Up to four pets for day visits and up to five for overnight stays.

Which areas do you serve?

The Fairview Meadows neighborhood. I keep my service area small so every pet gets close, personal attention. Referrals available on request.

Do you board pets at your home?

No. All care happens in your home, where your pet is most comfortable, including overnight stays.

What do I need to provide?

Your pet's food and any medications, along with instructions for feeding, schedule, or special needs.

How does pricing work?

Rates depend on the number of pets, length of stay, and whether you need overnight care. Day visits start at $50 and overnight stays are $100 per day.

Can we meet before I book?

Yes. I'm happy to set up a visit beforehand so you and your pet can meet me and see how we get along.
